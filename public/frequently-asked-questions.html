Account, login and OTP

Q: Does the project use two-step verification?
A: Yes. Faculty, Student, and Leadership logins require password plus email OTP verification.
Q: What if OTP does not arrive?
A: Check spam/junk, verify SMTP settings on the server, then use OTP resend on the login page.

Camera and live streaming

Q: Why is the camera preview black?
A: Grant browser camera permission, ensure no other app is locking the camera, and refresh the page once.
Q: Why is student stream not visible in faculty dashboard?
A: Start camera on student page, then start streaming; ensure faculty dashboard is online and session is active.

Camera controls (faculty dashboard — interactive demo)

The faculty dashboard uses the same layout for the student live stream: digital zoom, optional auto face zoom (runs only in your browser), and reset view. Use your own camera below to try it. Nothing is recorded or sent to the server from this demo.

Camera view — zoom & auto framing (same controls as faculty)

Tap “Start camera preview” to enable controls. On the real faculty page, these apply to the student’s live video.

Attendance rules

Q: Why was attendance marked absent?
A: Attendance considers hand raise / attention / face presence windows. If required checks are not met within the window, status becomes absent.
Q: Can attendance be restricted to campus only?
A: Yes. Enable campus CIDR enforcement via environment configuration on the backend.

AI attention and pulse

Q: How is attention score generated?
A: The student page computes gaze/posture-based score locally and sends only anonymized numeric attention signals.
Q: Why is pulse not updating?
A: Ensure there is an active session, a student stream is running, and faculty dashboard has selected the active session.

Class engagement map

Q: What does the map represent?
A: It is a zone-wise classroom engagement heatmap (front/middle/back zones) based on recent attention trends.

Security and privacy

Q: Is data protected?
A: The system uses session security, password hashing, OTP verification, route rate limits, and optional HTTPS deployment hardening.
Q: Are credentials exposed in UI?
A: No. Credentials are handled server-side and OTP is time-limited for verification.

Faculty dashboard — REC Smart Assist voice commands

Q: How do I use voice on the faculty dashboard?
A: Click the microphone button (bottom-right) or say a wake phrase such as “REC Smart Assist”, then speak your command in English. Recognition runs in the browser; nothing is sent to the server as voice audio.
Q: What commands are supported?
A: Examples — Open or close REC Smart Assist chat; open FAQs in a new tab; show notifications or innovation highlights; scroll to attendance or session details; refresh active sessions; start or end session (same as the dashboard buttons); refresh AI agent or attendance table; read attention / pulse; focus front, middle, or back row (camera hints); hardware zoom camera; student stream digital zoom: “zoom in”, “zoom out”, “reset view”, “auto face zoom” / “auto face zoom off”; confusion zone hints. Anything else is answered by the AI assistant when possible.
Q: Does voice work in every browser?
A: Use a current Chromium-based browser (Chrome or Edge) for best speech recognition. Allow microphone permission when prompted.
